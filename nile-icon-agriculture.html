About Us

Nile-Icon Agriculture is a community-focused agricultural organization based in South Sudan. We are committed to combating hunger and ensuring food security through sustainable farming, education, and empowerment of local communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to eradicate hunger in South Sudan by:

  • Promoting sustainable farming practices
  • Supporting local farmers with tools and training
  • Creating access to nutritious food for all communities
  • Empowering women and youth through agricultural education
